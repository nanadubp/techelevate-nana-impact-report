Our Current Programs

Four pathways helping learners gain skills, confidence, and access in the digital world.

Program 1

Coding for Students (Ages 8–14)

Free, hands-on coding classes that guide learners from first shapes to their own apps and games.

8–16 week sessions • Certificates earned
Program 2

Digital Literacy for Seniors

Interactive workshops that build confidence with internet safety, scams, devices, and AI.

45-minute sessions • 75+ seniors served
Program 3

Technology Assistance

Laptops and hotspots provided to students who need reliable access to learn.

Devices shipped free to learners
Program 4

Robotics Workshops

Creative block-coding sessions where kids build and program robots at local libraries.

Hosted regularly throughout the year
Every program is free, community-based, and designed to remove barriers to participation.
